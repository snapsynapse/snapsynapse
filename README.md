### Sam Rogers

Building open infrastructure for human-AI collaboration.
Founder & CEO, [PAICE.work](https://paice.work/) · President, [Snap Synapse](https://snapsynapse.com/)

Most AI adoption fails at the seams between roles, decisions, and operating models. I build measurement frameworks, open protocols, and agent tooling that make collaboration between humans and AI systems structurally sound, not just technically possible.

**Current focus:** Shipping [PAICE](https://paice.work/) behavioral diagnostics for People+AI Collaboration Effectiveness.

#### What I'm building

These are open standards and tooling born from [PAICE](https://paice.work/) and released independently because interoperability is the point, not lock-in.

**Protocols & specifications**

| Project | What it does |
|---|---|
| [graceful-boundaries](https://github.com/snapsynapse/graceful-boundaries) | Spec for how services communicate operational limits to humans and agents |
| [turnfile](https://github.com/snapsynapse/turnfile) | File-based protocol for LLM agents to negotiate and collaborate without an orchestrator |
| [hardguard25](https://github.com/snapsynapse/hardguard25) | 25-character alphabet for human-friendly unique IDs |

**Agent skills**

| Project | What it does |
